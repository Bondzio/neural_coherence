<s> Since I'm commenting, I should add that your choice of registered memory is not the wisest one... it's expensive and limits your choices... only servers use registered RAM, apart from the Athlon64 FX-51. </s>
<s> Unbuffered RAM is definitely better and cheaper. </s>
<s> Your 3200+ has a 1mb L2 cache... meaning that its a Clawhammer from the 754 socket generation. </s>
<s> It's not a good upgrade path and may be phased out after the nForce 4/K8T890 boards are released. </s>
<s> Furthermore, it uses a single channel memory controller instead of the 939's dual channel controller. </s>
<s> Thus, it's performance isn't nearly as good as the 939 socket processors. </s>
