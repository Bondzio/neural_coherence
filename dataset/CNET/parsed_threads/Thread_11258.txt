<s> A router is a Layer 3 device in the OSI model. </s>
<s> It connects two networks with different IP networks. </s>
<s> It makes forwarding decisions based on the IP address. </s>
<s> A bridge or switch is a layer 2 device (OSI) and it makes forwarding decisions based on the hardware address (MAC address) Most consumer routers are effectively a 2 port router (LAN side & WAN side). </s>
<s> The LAN interface is usually attached to a 4 port switch. </s>
<s> A wireless Access Point is a bridge that connects the wired to the unwired devices. </s>
<s> The AP is an extension of the 4 port switch side of the router. </s>
