<s> Everything looks fine in the settings, I have even tried resetting defaults. </s>
<s> PC works fine when resuming, but the boot process stops until F1 or F10 is pressed. </s>
<s> I installed the 2nd hard drive to move from old PC (P90 Dell) to the Compaq. </s>

<s> mrepos </s>
<s> 233 MHz computers may use older ISA modem cards which you set the serial port to COM1 or such. </s>
<s> When you do this, you learn that the motherboard port at the same address may need to be disabled. </s>
<s> Bob </s>
<s> I disabled COM2 in System Properties. </s>
<s> Error gone!!!! </s>

<s> Excellent resolution to this POST/BIOS error after switching in some newer hardware. </s>
