<s> You need to unplug the device from your computer before you uninstall the driver in device manager. </s>
<s> As long as the device is plugged into your computer, it will continue to appear in device manager, even if you try to delete it from there. </s>
<s> Or you can try looking for the device in the &quot;Safely Remove Hardware&quot; area in the system tray and uninstall it from there. </s>
<s> If you still get the &quot;found new hardware&quot; message after you try these methods, then you should allow Windows to go ahead and install the driver again. </s>
<s> After that, you should be able to see the device in device manager instead of seeing &quot;unknown device&quot; with a question mark.Once the device has been restored to the device manager without that error message, you can then go ahead and uninstall the driver for good. </s>
<s> If you have narrowed the device down to the APC UPS, why not install the correct driver from www.apc.com ? </s>
<s> Try the following link and download the appropiate UPS software for your particular UPS. </s>
