<s> I have a SinglePoint modem (PCI Internal, 56 kbps, V.92 Fax Modem), the modem model name is CN5614RS. </s>
<s> My problem is, when I have a connection to Internet the modem sounds a lot, I mean ALL the time and I dont know how to make it silent. </s>
<s> Perhapse someone can help me so solve the noise problem. </s>
<s> This should work in Windows 98, ME or XP, so it doesn't matter what you're running (In the future, though, please let us know your operating ststem). </s>
<s> 1. </s>
<s> Right click My Computer, select Properties. </s>
<s> 2. </s>
<s> Click on Hardware tab, click on Device Manager. </s>
<s> 3. </s>
<s> Expand Modems entry by clicking on the + sign, right click your modem and select Properties. </s>
<s> 4. </s>
<s> Click the Modem tab and slide the Speaker Volume adjustment to Off (all the way to the left). </s>
<s> Click OK and close the window. </s>
<s> This should shut off your modem's speaker. </s>
