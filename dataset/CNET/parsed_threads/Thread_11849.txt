<s> Hey I have a problem, I did not set a date for my system restore when it was new, so how can I restore it to its original state. </s>
<s> Please any help would be appreciated. </s>
<s> System Restore creates restore Points on a regular basis. </s>
<s> Go to start/all programs/accessories/system tools/system restore and double click. </s>
<s> Select restore my computer to an earlier time. </s>
<s> You can use the arrow keys to go back to earliwer months. </s>
<s> Find the point you want and click next and follow the prompts from there. </s>
