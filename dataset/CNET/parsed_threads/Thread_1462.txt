<s> found this on another forum and it worked for my Dell 1501, hope it works or you. </s>
<s> Disable the ATI HotKey Poller service in Windows. </s>
<s> Go to Start-&gt;Control Panel-&gt;Administrative Tools-&gt;Services You should see the ATI HotKey Poller Services listed. </s>
<s> Right Click on it and select properties. </s>
<s> Halfway down the screen you should see the &quot;Startup type.&quot; Choose disabled from the drop-down list, click apply, close out all applications and reboot. </s>
<s> This should resolve your issues. </s>
