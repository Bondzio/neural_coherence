<s> Bought an Ibuypower 528-GN, through Tiger Direct, on 18 Dec 08. </s>
<s> System arrived on 8 Jan 09. </s>
<s> Video kept locking up. </s>
<s> Screen would freeze or go black, using both video card and on-board vga outputs. </s>
<s> Troubleshot with local experts (after no response from Ibuypower tech support) and discovered the Mobo was bad. </s>
<s> Got replacement Asus M2A-VM from Ibuypower, and that was bad too. </s>
<s> Perhaps IBuypower got a bad batch of Asus M2A-VMs, and I got two of them. </s>
<s> Bought an Asus M3A78-CM Mobo, installed, and system works fine now. </s>
<s> I tried getting IBuypower tech support from 9 Jan to early Feb, and gave up. </s>
<s> Just some words to the wise. </s>
<s> Good luck out there. </s>
