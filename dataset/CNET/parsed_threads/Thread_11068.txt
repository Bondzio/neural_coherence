<s> I am also a Dell Owner and recently installed an IDE in addition to my Serial ATA. </s>
<s> I began receiving a similar error but only after powering off the machine. </s>
<s> A restart would go cleanly so it appeared sporadic. </s>
<s> You will probably be able to duplicate this after a complete shutdown. </s>
<s> Anyway, I will make a couple assumptions. </s>
<s> 1) You are installing a Serial ATA in addition to your Primary IDE Hard Drive. </s>
<s> Here is how I believe I resolved my issue. </s>
<s> Go into Bios - Press F2 for setup. </s>
<s> Serial ATA will probably say say Yes - Press the Space Bar and turn it Off. </s>
<s> You should see the Bios request status for two ATA drives. </s>
<s> Under your Serial ATA Drive 1 - set it to Auto ( so that it will autodetect on bootup.) </s>
<s> After rebooting, you should get a message 'new hardware found' Shutdown and reboot the system. </s>
<s> Your F1/F2 to continue error should go away because you are forcing your system to recognize the hard drive in question and not confuse it with other. </s>
<s> If this still doesn't work, a slightly riskier steup would be to set your Primary IDE Drive in Bios to Auto and make sure all Other IDE Drives (Primary/Secondary) are set to OFF. </s>
<s> Here is a link from Western Digital to look at for checking your jumper settings on your primary IDE drive. </s>
