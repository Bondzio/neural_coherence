Hurricane Georges , with winds up to 135 mph , plowed over and past 17 Caribbean islands for 11 days in September 1998 , causing billions of dollars in destruction and killing at least 500 people . The numbers of homeless are staggering , with more than 100,000 people left homeless in the Dominican Republic alone . U.S. taxpayers are sure to foot most of the bill in Puerto Rico , where 30,000 homes were destroyed and another 60,000 damaged . The Caribbean tourist industry 's position remained good since many important tourist destinations were spared . The varied island societies prepared and reacted to Hurricane Georges in radically different ways .
