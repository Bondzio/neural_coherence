<s> Aside from codecs... two basic questions: 1. </s>
<s> Is the DVD you're using pre-recorded or is it a home-grown version? </s>
<s> If home-grown, was it finalized on the original recorder before you attempted to play it on the computer? </s>
<s> Unless it's finalized, it won't be readible by any other player but the one it was recorded on. </s>
<s> 2. </s>
<s> If this is a blank DVD, are you sure it's compatible with your drive? </s>
<s> (DVD + vs. -, 1x-8x format, ROM vs. RAM, etc.) </s>
<s> Some media just won't work with certain recorders. </s>
<s> A good place to go for compatibility charts and help in general is www.dvdrhelp.com Good luck. </s>
