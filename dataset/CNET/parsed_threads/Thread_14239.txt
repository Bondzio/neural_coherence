<s> They came from a locked media, CD, or because the ownership of them has changed. </s>
<s> You initially told us that you were only the &quot;manager&quot; on your OS X server, where the files on the CD were created. </s>
<s> Now that you are the owner and administrator of the OS X machine, things have changed as regards those files on the CD. </s>
<s> Just drag them onto the HD of your machine, select them all, get info and remove the check mark from the &quot;Locked&quot; box. </s>
<s> Close the window. </s>
<s> Everything should be alright now. </s>
<s> If, on the OS X files, you still find yourself unable to read or write to them, drag them to the HD, select them all, get info and then change ownership by changing the Read/Write status of the Owner. </s>
<s> Read everything in the get info box and you will see this option towards the bottom, on the left hand side. </s>
<s> If the options are grayed out, click the padlock and enter your administrators password when prompted. </s>
