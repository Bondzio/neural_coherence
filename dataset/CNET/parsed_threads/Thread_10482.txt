<s> Did you connect it on it's own cable or on a cable already used by another drive? </s>
<s> If on the same cable as an existing drive you need to set it as a slave device. </s>
<s> Jumper settings are marked on the drive. </s>
<s> Did you check that the cable is connected the right way? </s>
<s> red or black tracer wire nearest the power connector. </s>
<s> Did you connect a power connector to the drive? </s>
<s> No drives showing tends to indicate no power or incorrect cable installation. </s>
<s> Check all connections and jumper settings and let's know how you get on. </s>

<s> I connected its own cable and connected it to a DVD drive already installed. </s>
<s> Both of the drives previously installed were set to CSEL, so I set the Sony drive to that as well. </s>
<s> The cables are connected correctly and power connectors are powering the drives. </s>
<s> Should I use the previous IDE cable that was connected to the previous drive? </s>
<s> you used the new cable that came with the drive and connected it to the existing drive. </s>
<s> In that case disconnect it and reconnect the drive to the cable that is connected on the old drive (the center connection) and set it as a slave drive. </s>
<s> If you have two such cables (PATA connections)one of which is connected to the hard drive and have a free center connection (secondary drive) use the connection that goes to the hard drive. </s>
<s> It's better to have the CD/DVD drives on separate IDE connections. </s>
<s> Hope this solves your problem, let's know how you get on, John </s>
<s> the new CDRW drive may have come with a 40 wire connection and the DVD that was in your machine may need an 80 wire cable so use the cable that was fitted originally. </s>
<s> If cable select doesn't work set the drive on the end as master and other as slave, If it's a cable that has a blue connector at one end and a black at the other, make sure to plug the blue end of the cable to the IDE channel on the motherboard. </s>
