sli is 2 or even 3 dedicated graphics cards.
you cannot bridge the onboard graphic chipset with an add-on graphics card for dual support.
in fact, you would disable the onboard graphics chip in order to use a dedicated video card.
also, most motherboards in store-bought systems are not high-end, but usually entry-level motherboards.
an important consideration to look at before buying one of these systems is whether it even has room to add a video card in ... some don't.
