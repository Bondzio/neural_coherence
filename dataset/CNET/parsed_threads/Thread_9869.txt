<s> a router and maybe a switch. </s>
<s> Your modem connects to a router. </s>
<s> The router has two basic functions. </s>
<s> One is that it acts as a hardware firewall and the other is that it can pass out IP addresses to connected devices. </s>
<s> If you have more devices than your router has ports, you connect a switch to the router. </s>
<s> The switch can usually be of the un-managed type unless you have a special need such as to dedicate a certain amount of bandwidth to some device Each device must connect to it's own port. </s>
<s> You cannot wire any of them together if that's what you mean by a splitter. </s>
<s> No splitting allowed. </s>
<s> You did say something about telephone lines. </s>
<s> Are these standard telephones or do you have bundled phone service on your cable? </s>
<s> Otherwise, these have nothing to do with your network. </s>
<s> As for a patch panel, you really don't need one unless you're using the stiffer interior wall type Ethernet cable for the house wiring. </s>
