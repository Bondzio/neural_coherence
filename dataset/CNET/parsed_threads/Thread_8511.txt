<s> I decided not to take the card apart and got my head inside the case again this time when system was powered on and running GTA4 at max. </s>
<s> I noticed the noise came specifically from the rear end of the card near the fan where the 6 pin power supply enters. </s>
<s> On the XFX version of this card there are no fixing points on the plastic cover to attach it to the card, even though the card has 2 holes at its rear. </s>
<s> Not a design fault Im sure just different companies use different fan/cover assemblies. </s>
<s> Anyway there is a gap between card and cover so I gently squeezed them together and the noise stopped! </s>
<s> I contemplated a few ideas to solve this problem. </s>
<s> Screws wouldnt work because nothing to screw into, a small G-clamp bit excessive and could damage the card, electrical tape good but after time tape comes loose. </s>
<s> I elected for elastic bands. </s>
<s> I bought a packet of bands 1cm width and 9cm long, a little shorter than the card width to allow for stretching. </s>
<s> I wrapped 2 around the rear of the card making sure they did not come near the fan. </s>
<s> Now all is quietquieter, all I hear now is the CPU fan. </s>
<s> So that was my quick fix which cost 1.50. </s>
