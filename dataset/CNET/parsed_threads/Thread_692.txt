<s> In short, I know this issue. </s>
<s> Today's printer port speeds may be too fast for this old printers. </s>
<s> The timing of the printer port was increased years ago and some printers just don't like it. </s>
<s> Fixes are: 1. </s>
<s> Get a NEW SHINY IEEE printer cable. </s>
<s> Some get uppity about such and insist the old cable is fine. </s>
<s> I'm not here to debate this, just share my fixes. </s>
<s> 2. </s>
<s> If the cable doesn't cure it, then we put in a PCI Printer Port card. </s>
<s> This works since most (in fact all) that I've used have the old Centronic's timing. </s>
<s> If this does not cure it, then it's time to get a new printer. </s>
