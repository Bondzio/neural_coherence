<s> A wireless router can only talk to devices that appear to be a end device. </s>
<s> To get a 2 routers to talk to each other you must set one to client mode or bridge mode. </s>
<s> Although more routers are starting to have this feature it is not common. </s>
<s> Check your router to see if it has this feature but they still tend to be tricky to setup because of the security. </s>
<s> There are devices made to do this called wireless adapters or wireless extenders but those terms are used fairly loosely. </s>
