<s> The wires looking burnt is not good. </s>
<s> As Robert stated to you, there too much current draw on the wires. </s>
<s> Either the wires are badly designed/manufacture or how the PC is being used as in devices connected maybe too much. </s>
<s> You gave no clue to the wattage rating but if the PC is using a less than capable PSU it will push it until it fails. </s>
<s> -OR- the connection &quot;crimps&quot; is badly done or wiring selected is less-than-desirable &quot;gauge&quot; it overdrives its safety rating. </s>
<s> Basically, it can only get worse over time. </s>
<s> Replace the PSU with name brand of decent wattage that your PC can use with 100W to spare. </s>
<s> Also, sometimes a poorly seated PSU cable can cause a bad connection, but that's rare. </s>
<s> Alas, swap out the PSU for now, so if you have a 500W PSU get a 600W one. </s>
<s> Make sure that PC is well cooled. </s>
