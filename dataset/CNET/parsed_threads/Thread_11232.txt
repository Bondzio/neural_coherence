<s> If you can find any tell tale marking on the board as to manufactuer and model, manuals are often available on line. </s>
<s> Basically, you will have a power (two with older AT styles) and front panel connections to make. </s>
<s> Not many of these are necessary. </s>
<s> Some MBs have all of these in a cluster with some silkscreening that identifies what goes where and the front panel connectors are marked. </s>
<s> You will have a reset and speaker connection plus some for power LEDs. </s>
<s> LED connections have polarity requirements. </s>
<s> Reset switches and speaker connections do not. </s>
<s> If your board is an ATX and has one larger (usually 20 pin dual inline) connector, you will need to connect the power switch to the proper connector on the MB. </s>
<s> Try to find info about the board by marking on it an post back. </s>
<s> If MB is AT (two power cables often) all the black wires (grounds) should wind up in the middle. </s>
