<s> I'm getting Bad Sectors, Clusters, and/or Missing Allocations what should I do? </s>
<s> If it's a diskette or CD or DVD or USB-stick or memory card: throw away without trying anything. </s>
<s> If it's a hard disk: format. </s>
<s> If that results in more than 0 errors or bad sectors or such, throw away also. </s>
<s> That's the most likely outcome. </s>
<s> If no errors left, distrust the hard disk in the future and be very careful to have excellent backup of what it contains. </s>
<s> Throwing away includes physical destruction if the medium contains private information. </s>
