<s> Specifics A Dell Windows Me with a Maxtor 5TO60H6 hard drive A Hp Pavilion a367c Windows Xp </s>
<s> Jumper old 60GB HD as slave. </s>
<s> Install in slave position on data cable and insert power connector. </s>
<s> Turn on computer. </s>
<s> Enter BIOS before windows starts. </s>
<s> Make sure the original HP Pavilion HD is primary boot drive. </s>
<s> Exit BIOS. </s>
<s> Let Windows start normally. </s>
<s> Secondary 60GB drive should show in My Computer. </s>
<s> GOOD IDEA: Move all important personal files (docs, pics, music, etc.) </s>
<s> to HP hard drive, and then format old 60GB drive from DISK MANAGEMENT. </s>
<s> That will give you a clean 60 GB HD for storage. </s>
