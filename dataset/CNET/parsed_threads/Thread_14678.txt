<s> Depending on the rest of her setup, you don't need the printer brands hardware. </s>
<s> She will need a wireless network card....either mini PCI if available or using an available add in slot (pcmcia types). </s>
<s> Something with 802.11 b/g should be fine. </s>
<s> These can be bought for under $50. </s>
<s> Wireless print servers come in parallel and USB types and tend to run about $80 and up though bargains can be found. </s>
<s> If this is within your price range, options are many. </s>
<s> Some print servers just don't work with certain printers. </s>
