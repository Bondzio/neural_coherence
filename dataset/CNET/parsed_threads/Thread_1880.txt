<s> Now when I use it for longer than 15 minutes or so I hear a repeated whirring about two seconds in duration: whir...whir...whir. </s>
<s> It's very annoying. </s>
<s> I don't have a cd or any other media in any of the drives. </s>
<s> The computer is about a year old, and I haven't had any other problem with it so far. </s>
<s> Any ideas? </s>
<s> If its only a year old and still under warranty, take it in for a check up. </s>
<s> Otherwise it sounds like a fan might be going bad. </s>
<s> Sometimes you can take off the cover plate and determine which fan it might be. </s>
<s> Something may have been knocked loose also during the move and maybe just needs a little adjusting. </s>
<s> This might simply be what is called a &quot;sympathetic&quot; vibration which is a chassis noise that comes and goes as it is vibrated by a motor from a fan or drive. </s>
<s> It could also be a &quot;harmonic&quot; created by the difference in frequency of two motors (again fans/drives) that becomes a third noise when these other frequencies &quot;beat&quot; against each other. </s>
<s> You need to disconnect one motor device (except for the CPU fan) at a time to see if it goes away. </s>
<s> That it happens after about 15 minutes might have something to do with things warming up and reaching full speed. </s>
<s> Good luck. </s>
