<s> I also have a zd8000, and there's a really easy way to tell. </s>
<s> If you look on the side of the computer with drive (should be the left-hand side), there should be 3 icons on the drive door. </s>
<s> The first one (towards the back of the computer) should be the DVD symbol, the middle one should be the CD symbol (says &quot;disc&quot;), and the one towards the front should say &quot;RW&quot;. </s>
<s> Underneath the very front one, it will say &quot;DVD+R DL&quot; in very small letters if you have a double-layer drive. </s>
<s> I believe it came standard on all zd8000's, but I'm not sure. </s>
