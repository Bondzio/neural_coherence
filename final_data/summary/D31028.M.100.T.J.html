Hurricane Georges , packing winds up to 110 mph , swept through the northern Caribbean killing over 400 people , leaving at least 140,000 homeless , and causing billions in property damage . In Puerto Rico alone , though the residents were forewarned of the hurricane 's approach , nearly 30,000 houses were destroyed . The cost of rebuilding the homes will be largely borne by the residents and U.S. taxpayers , since most homeowners are uninsured . Haiti , the poorest nation in the Western hemisphere was a disaster zone . There , mudslides and floods killed a total of 221 people , 102 in Fonds Verettes . Georges , however , spared many other Caribbean tourist destinations .
