<s> With computers so low priced for what you want it for I would say buy new. </s>
<s> If you want to have it checked out find a computer shop with a low bench fee as a final check. </s>
<s> I did this and for 35 US dollars was told what I had already guessed. </s>
<s> There could be a cheap fix for the Persario but you may spend more time and energy fixing it than it is worth. </s>
<s> If it's your hobby go for it. </s>
<s> By all means pull HDD (security reasons). </s>
<s> But the ram may not be usable in a newer machine. </s>
<s> I would pull ram if I knew what type/spec. </s>
<s> Label all parts and bag'em (anti-static bag or leave in case). </s>
<s> You can recycle the drive as a slave in another computer. </s>
<s> Please understand that the drive may be fried as well so don't lose any sleep over it. </s>
