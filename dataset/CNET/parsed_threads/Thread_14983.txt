<s> You said it was an old machine and you'd been using the on board video. </s>
<s> Obviously there's a possibility of a driver problem. </s>
<s> As Bob says, set your refresh and resolution to something safe. </s>
<s> But, what I'd do is make sure the on board video is disabled and I'd uninstall the Radeon card from the display adapters in your hardware. </s>
<s> It will show two displays...one is analog and the other is digital. </s>
<s> Get rid of both. </s>
<s> As well, go to control panel /Add Remove and look for any ATI software installation such as it's Catalyst suite and get rid of it. </s>
<s> Download the current drivers from the manufacturer's web site. </s>
<s> ATI is now an AMD owned company so go there. </s>
<s> Don't get the Catalyst suite. </s>
<s> Just get the driver and install it first. </s>
<s> If you feel the Catalyst extras are worth it, you can get that later once everything is working. </s>
<s> I don't care for it. </s>
<s> One issue you could have is if your card wasn't made by ATI but one of their &quot;partners&quot;. </s>
<s> There were many. </s>
<s> Time was when you had to look for reference drivers for such cards as installing the OEM drivers resulted in errors. </s>
<s> Hopefully that won't be the case with yours. </s>
<s> Good luck. </s>
