(11/25/1998) CAIRO, Egypt (AP) _ Newspapers reported on 11/25/1998 that three top Libyan officials have been tried and jailed in the Lockerbie case, but Libyan dissidents said the reports appeared to be a political ploy by Libya's leader, Col. Moammar Gadhafi. (11/25/1998) The reported jailing of the three officials comes as Gadhafi is under pressure to accept a plan to turn over for trial two other Libyans wanted for the 1988 bombing of Pan Am flight 103 over Lockerbie, Scotland, that led to 270 deaths. (11/25/1998) The Tripoli government has insisted that the suspects serve any prison time in Libya or the Netherlands.
