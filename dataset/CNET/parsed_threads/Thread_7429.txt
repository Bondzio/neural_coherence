<s> Think of the broadcast range of the wireless router as a big bubble with the router in its center. </s>
<s> You will likely find that you will maximize your wireless coverage if you place ther wireless router on the second floor, or maybe the first floor. </s>
<s> You can make a wired connection to the wireless router via a network cable. </s>
<s> This MUST be done for the initial setup of the router. </s>
<s> Any subsequent wireless connections will be from compouters that have WIRELESS NETWORK ADAAPTERS. </s>
<s> If a computer doesn't have a wireless network adapter, you can't make a wireless network connection. </s>
<s> There are internal wireless network adapters and external adapters. </s>
<s> Most newer notebooks come with an internal wireless adapter. </s>
<s> This is a configuration that I would not suggest for a beginner to home networking. </s>
<s> Having two DHCP serving devices on the same network segment requires you to turn off that function on one usually the wired router so it serves as a switch only. </s>
<s> Look into your wired router's setup page and jot down the settings. </s>
<s> Then replace it with the wireless router and duplicate those settings in it's setup program. </s>
