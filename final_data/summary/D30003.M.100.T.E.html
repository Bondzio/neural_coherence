Former Chilean dictator Gen. Augusto Pinochet was arrested in London on charges of human rights violations . Pinochet , in London for back surgery , was arrested in a Spanish warrant accusing him of genocide and terrorism . It is alleged that Pinochet was responsible for the disappearance or death of over 3,000 people while he was dictator of Chile between 1973 and 1900 . Chile protested the arrest stating that Pinochet 's status as senator-for-life gave him diplomatic immunity . Britain 's Labor government was obviously embarrassed by the diplomatic implications of the arrest . The arrest shows the growing significance of international human rights law .
