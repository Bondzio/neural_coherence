Terrorist bombings of the U.S. Embassies in Nairobi , Kenya and Dar es Salaam , Tanzania on August 7 1998 killed 248 in Nairobi , and 11 in Dar es Salaam . In Nairobi 5400 were injured and 97 fully or partially blinded . In the 76 days following the bombings evidence was gathered linking the perpetrators to the Al Qaeda organization of Osama bin Laden , several indictments were made and several suspects were arrested but no one had yet been brought to trial . A key figure in the Nairobi bombing , Abdallah Mohammed Fazul , head of Al Qaeda in Kenya , had disappeared and was still at large .
