<s> Welcome to the world of advertising and perfect laboratory conditions. </s>
<s> There are so many factors involved with signal strength and bit speed, it's not worth describing. </s>
<s> In a nutshell, distance and obstructions are the determining factor. </s>
<s> In perfect laboratory conditions you will get 105 Mb/s. </s>
<s> Won't ever happen in the real world. </s>
<s> As most people don't realize, higher frequencies can travel farther, but are more susceptible to interference and blocking. </s>
<s> Lower frequencies travel through most anything, but speed is affected. </s>
<s> Consider ULF (ultra low frequency) that the Navy uses to travel hundreds of miles under water, at a bit rate of about 10. </s>
<s> The higher the frequency, the more obstructions (walls) interfere. </s>
