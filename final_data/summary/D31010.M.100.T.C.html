Beginning November 16 , a frigid Arctic air mass brought unusually cold weather lasting several weeks to most of Europe . Below normal temperatures were experienced in France , Romania , Bulgaria , Poland and Russia . Rare snowfall occurred in southern Italy . Throughout Poland , Europe 's most affected country , the death toll reached 85 . Most deaths were blamed on homelessness and drinking alcohol . In Russian , at least 39 people died in Moscow alone ; an addition 143 people were hospitalized with hypothermia and 51 with frostbite . Throughout Europe transportation was disrupted and weather related accidents occurred . The prolonged sub-zero temperatures made this Europe 's coldest November in many , many years .
