<s> How to access, modify, or exit the BIOS setup utility on a desktop computer. </s>
<s> The BIOS setup utility is accessed using a key combination before the computer starts the operating system. </s>
<s> Restart the computer. </s>
<s> At the initial Sony screen, on the keyboard, press the F2 key to enter the BIOS setup utility. </s>
<s> Press the ARROW keys to navigate through the menus. </s>
<s> To display a sub menu in the BIOS setup utility, highlight the menu item and then press the ENTER key. </s>
<s> Press the PLUS (+) or MINUS (-) keys to modify the BIOS setup values. </s>
<s> Press the F10 key to exit the BIOS setup utility. </s>
<s> In the Setup Confirmation dialog box, press the ENTER key to save the changes and exit. </s>
