<s> Stupid forum will not cleanly let me post the specs on your switch so. </s>
<s> You only have 9 ports. </s>
<s> The last port is shared with the SFP module. </s>
<s> This is very common trick vendors do when counting ports. </s>
<s> The 10th port can either be the built in port or the SFP. </s>
<s> Both are not active. </s>
<s> In your case the last port is not even POE so should put your router in port 10 In general I do not think I have seen a SFP that can do PoE itself. </s>
<s> They can do copper 10/100/1000 but not the PoE. </s>
<s> Many times in switches like this they do not support the copper SFP module since its silly to do it since the built in port is already 10/100/1000 copper. </s>
<s> I think you are out of luck you are going to have to daisy chain another switch to make this work. </s>
