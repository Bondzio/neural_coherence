<s> Yes, additional systems would benefit from the router-based firewall. </s>
<s> At the same time, it may still be worth having a firewall on each PC. </s>
<s> The only cavaet I would note with a router-based firewall is that if you set it up fairly tightly, you may be surprised at how many holes it'll have to allow for various programs to run, e.g. </s>
<s> IMs and other software can use special ports that you'd have to open if your firewall configuration is really tight while others can have them be pretty loose. </s>
<s> Last but not least, a router-based firewall may not have all the logging features that a software firewall has in terms of logging intrusions so that you can see who was pinging you or whatever it was that hit your router. </s>
