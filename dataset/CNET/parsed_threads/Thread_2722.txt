<s> thermal compound, between it and the CPU surface. </s>
<s> Starting with absolutely clean surfaces, the thermal compound needs to be only a rather thin film. </s>
<s> No way is more goop better. </s>
<s> If you select a conductive paste then be careful to not get it anywhere but on the actual mating surfaces. </s>
<s> It is NOT a squeeze out between the two surfaces process. </s>
<s> Keep in mind that the interior ambient air temperature that the HSF is transfering heat to should be only about 12 to 20 F degrees above your room ambient. </s>
<s> Therefore cooling the entire interior will also lower CPU temps. </s>
<s> Yep Athlons run hot. </s>
<s> If it is one that does NOT have the heat spreader to increase the effective surface of the CPU, the heat is being dissipated over about a one square inch surface. </s>
<s> Thus the thermal transfer from the CPU to the Heatsink is very critical. </s>
<s> Intel has used the heat speader for years. </s>
<s> I understand that some of the 32 bit Athlons are now using them. </s>
<s> The AMD 64 bitters all use heatspreaders. </s>
