<s> With an unknown video display, Windows won't know its capabilities and will use some default settings. </s>
<s> When you change these, they may not stick as Windows will rediscover the same unknown display device each time it starts. </s>
<s> Your first quest should be to identify the display device and find a proper driver. </s>
<s> If possible, you also look for a driver for the monitor. </s>
<s> Once everyone gets to know each other, they work better together. </s>
