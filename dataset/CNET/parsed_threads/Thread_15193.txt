<s> One reason some opt for the wide screen is for watching DVD movies because the aspect ratios are similar. </s>
<s> It might also have some advantage if you edit movies for recording on DVDs or even for some photo editing jobs, or for viewing wide spreadsheets. </s>
<s> these advantages obtain, however, only if you are dealing with a true widescreen version of the same basic standard monitor. </s>
<s> But in this case, both of the monitors have the same 1280 horizontal pixels and about the same dot pitch; they are essentially the same width but the widescreen has less height. </s>
<s> Thus I do not see any significant advantage of the widescreen for most ordinary work. </s>
<s> For example, you would not see any more columns of a spreadsheet, and would actually lose some rows. </s>
<s> If you work mainly with documents, the line length and visual character size would be the same and, again, you would lose some rows. </s>
<s> Actually, even watching movies would be little different. </s>
<s> The image would be the same size, there would just be some blank space at the top and bottom of the 19 inch monitor. </s>
