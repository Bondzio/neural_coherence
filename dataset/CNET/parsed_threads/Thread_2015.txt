<s> Since stating a minimum PSU never is a good spec I use a 20 buck Watt Meter. </s>
<s> I put the machine up on the Watt Meter and look at the readings at idle on the Windows screen then a few games. </s>
<s> Then I can figure out if we are in trouble. </s>
<s> Most PSU's are about 80% efficient so we know the Watts on the AC is that far off. </s>
<s> To see if we are in trouble I use 50% of the PSU's rating and don't want the Watt meter to exceed that. </s>
<s> This gives the PSU some head room. </s>
<s> ONE LAST CHECK is the Amperes on the PSU rail check. </s>
<s> You find the video card's amperes and make sure the PSU rails can support that. </s>
