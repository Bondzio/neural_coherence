<s> Your answer may be 2 drives. </s>
<s> One for Mac and the other for the PC. </s>
<s> Let me state that most external drives fail the basic tests for backup on these 2 points. </s>
<s> 1. </s>
<s> Write protect switch. </s>
<s> Most drives do not have a switch that turns the drive into a read only device. </s>
<s> 2. </s>
<s> The second copy (and third) copy of the backup. </s>
<s> Since most will have just one drive and will be writing to it there is no assurance that the virus or worm won't wipe it all out. </s>
