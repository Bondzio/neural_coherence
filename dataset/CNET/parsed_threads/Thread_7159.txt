<s> I suggest you reboot the whole shebang and the try the removal/delete of pending document to print before anything else. </s>
<s> If it fails then, then be sure the printer is acknowledged as default, etc., to be active, don't swap USB ports, return to actual port. </s>
<s> Otherwise, the printer port resets to #1, #2, start to collect, delete all those and reboot. </s>
<s> Now, when the USB port acknowledges the printer(default), then send to print document. </s>
<s> This problem is also present to non-networked printers. </s>
