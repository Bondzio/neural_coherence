<s> Beeps are coming from BIOS which means it has detected a hardware error. </s>
<s> It's often a memory or video issue but could be many things. </s>
<s> When this happens, there is no attempt to boot to any OS. </s>
<s> The PC must pass POST (power on self test) first. </s>
<s> The beep code is trying to tell you what is wrong but without a list of the codes it's hard to tell. </s>
<s> What you can do is reseat components such as the video card (AGPs are notorious for problems with poor seating in the card slot) and memory modules. </s>
<s> If you have more than one memory module, remove all but one and then try them individually to see if you can get the beeps to go away. </s>
<s> Good luck. </s>
