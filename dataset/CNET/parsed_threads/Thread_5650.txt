<s> Maxtor has a program that comes with their HDs called MaxBlast. </s>
<s> It will clone an existing drive to a new drive. </s>
<s> You install the software, then power off and install the drive as slave. </s>
<s> On bootup the software detects the new drive and will then (after a few prompts) copy/clone the new drive making a mirror image of the old drive. </s>
<s> Everything is saved. </s>
<s> You then remove the old drive, set the new drive to master and reboot. </s>
<s> I've used it many times. </s>
<s> If you're running XP you will probably be asked to reactivate but it's painless. </s>
