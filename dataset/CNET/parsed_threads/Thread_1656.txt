<s> The clock speed of a processor (Ghz/Mhz) may be misleading. </s>
<s> Different cpus do different amount of work per cycle. </s>
<s> Some processor may operate at a higher clock speed, but does less work per cycle, and ends up being slower than another processor that may be operating at a slower clock speed. </s>
<s> AMD cpus usually have the highest processing capacity per cycle, Celeron the lowest. </s>
<s> That's why many gamers choose AMD over Pentium, even though the fastest Pentium 4 processor operate at a much higher clock. </s>
<s> That said, Celeron looks good for your purposes. </s>
<s> It is by far the cheapest. </s>
