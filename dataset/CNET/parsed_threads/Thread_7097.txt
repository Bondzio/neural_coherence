<s> Ray According to Compaq, the 7598 uses the Intel 810 chipset. </s>
<s> You will have to install a PCI video card as there is no AGP slot on a 810 chipset motherboard. </s>
<s> Every 810 chipset motherboard that I have upgraded lets you disable the old display adapter in the Device Manager. </s>
<s> Bring up the Device Manager and click on the + next to Display Adapter. </s>
<s> Highlight your old video adapter and click Properties. </s>
<s> Look near the bottom of the properties window and find a square box labeled Exists in all hardware profiles. </s>
<s> Un-check this box. </s>
<s> That will disable the old video adapter. </s>
<s> Click OK. Make sure that you have installed your new PCI video card before you do this. </s>
<s> And after you install your new PCI video card, be sure to move the monitor cable to the new card. </s>
<s> No mention of any &quot;disable&quot; feature, but a select on which display would be primary. </s>
