<s> Remember that we are dealing with a dying machine so we can try everything and anything to avoid the motherboard. </s>
<s> For example I would unplug all CD/HD/DVD drives from the motherboard since we know an IDE short can stop the boot. </s>
<s> Same for ANY PCI or other card. </s>
<s> Remove what we can and get down to just the motherboard, power supply, CPU and heatsink. </s>
<s> If those don't start up and beep in protest for the lack of ram and more then we are looking at the bad parts. </s>
<s> Some &quot;test&quot; the power supply but since the RISE TIME of the voltages can cause odd boot issues I've learned to always swap it out. </s>
