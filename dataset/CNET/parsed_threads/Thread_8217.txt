<s> Okay, KUDOs to you TheDraac. </s>
<s> I got the drive fixed. </s>
<s> First I must have messed up the drive by using the Western Digital Lifeguard Tool on their own drive - ironic. </s>
<s> Using the Lifeguard Utility, I did what I had to and checked the BAs. </s>
<s> My Native MAX LBA did not match my Current MAX LBA, and I did not know how to change it. </s>
<s> I set, in the BIOS, the drive to LBA and the system got up to 137GB. </s>
<s> Then I ran the Acronis WD (Western Digital) Edition (free) to check the integrity of the drive. </s>
<s> It showed me the following partitions: 32GB, 137Gb and UNUSED 144GB. </s>
<s> I opened my Easeus Partition Master to check the drive and found all the full size - it gave me more than the factory, it gave me 610GB instead of the 595GB for a 640GB drive. </s>
