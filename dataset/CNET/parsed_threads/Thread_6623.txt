<s> First thing I would do, check the primary for issues. </s>
<s> 1. </s>
<s> Check your mobo website for newer BIOS versions which may have addressed any previous issues with IDE/SATA. </s>
<s> 2. </s>
<s> Try not to mess with default hard disk parms when configuring BIOS. </s>
<s> 3. </s>
<s> Ensure you install the correct mobo SATA driver (IDE emul, ACHI or RAID). </s>
<s> 4. </s>
<s> Finally for your own peace of mind: &gt; avoid installing apps to any drive other than C:\ &gt; never install apps to a drive you store precious data &gt; never use local storage containing irreplaceable programs/data as a source for program installation (install failure may cause whole source drive to be erased) </s>
