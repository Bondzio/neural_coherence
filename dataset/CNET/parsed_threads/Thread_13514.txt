<s> I am not sure if you are looking for indoor or outdoor use. </s>
<s> Keep in mind that the transmitter output power of home wireless equipment such as your Linksys router is very low and any gain that your Yagi antenna gives you will quickly be lost in long cable lengths. </s>
<s> If possible, mount the wireless router as close as possible to the antenna, even if it means that you need to put it in a waterproof box. </s>
<s> For power, you could run power over ethernet cable if needed. </s>
