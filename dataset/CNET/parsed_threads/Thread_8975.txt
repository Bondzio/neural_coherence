<s> How can I tell what version my USB is? </s>
<s> I have a USB device that requires USB 2.0 and I need to know if my oc is compatible. </s>
<s> I have checked the manufacturer's documentation, but they don't specify it anywhere. </s>
<s> Right click on My Computer on the Desktop. </s>
<s> Click Properties. </s>
<s> Click the Hardware tab, then Device Manager. </s>
<s> Scroll down to Universal Serial bus Controllers. </s>
<s> Click the + sign. </s>
<s> If you have a listing that says &quot;Standard Enhanced. </s>
<s> . </s>
<s> .&quot; you have USB-2. </s>
<s> If you don't have USB-2, USB-2 PCI boards are availble for around twenty bucks. </s>
