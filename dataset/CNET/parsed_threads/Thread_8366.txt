<s> Replacing a power supply is too easy. </s>
<s> Remove the case cover. </s>
<s> Unplug the power supply leads. </s>
<s> With a Phillips screwdriver remove the four mounting screws on the back. </s>
<s> Remove the PS. </s>
<s> Place the new PS in the same spot and replace the four screws. </s>
<s> Plug in the leads, they will only go one way. </s>
<s> Depending on the supply you may have leads left over. </s>
<s> Done. </s>
