With the arrest of political dissidents , including Xu Wenli , Wei Jingsheng , and Qin Yongman , China went on the offensive against individuals seeking to set up political parties in that country . In a speech to the Communist Party , Chinese President Jiang Zemin denounced any attempts to set up a multi-party system in China . The three prominent dissidents all were arrested on charges of subverting national security . With government harassment of lawyers , the dissidents have been unable to secure legan counsel and have had to defend themselves . The father of Qin Yongman has sought a delay in his son 's trial until legal counsel can be found .
