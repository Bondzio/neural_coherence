some of the smaller sized version of linux (puppy, dsl) can be installed on a flash drive.
i haven't heard anyone installing a version of windows onto a flash drive, but i would guess it's possible.
i'm sure people out there with far more knowledge than me would know of more advantages/disadvantages then i have listed below.
disadvantages: booting onto a flash drive is that it is much slower compared to a regular hard drive.
not all motherboards are capable of booting to a flash drive.
you would have check your bios to find out.
you are limited in your hard drive size.
advantages: you literally have everything in your pocket.
