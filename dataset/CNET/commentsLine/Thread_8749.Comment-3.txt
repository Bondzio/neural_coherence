your ddr-400 will run at ddr-400 regardless of capacity.
most mobo's have to run 4 sticks *slightly* slower (2t command rate) than 2 sticks (1t command).
however, two fast 1gb sticks may be more expensive than four equally fast 512mb sticks.
unless you plan on doing some *extreme* oc'ing, i'd go with 2 good quality 1gb sticks and tweaking them to lower the cas and/or boost the speed.
