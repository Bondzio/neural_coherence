(10/04/1998) CAIRO, Egypt _ In a sign of Arab concern over growing tensions between Turkey and Syria, Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak flew on short notice to Syria on 10/04/1998 in an effort to defuse what is being portrayed in the Middle East as an unsettling crisis. (10/04/1998) The differences between the two countries include a territorial dispute and arguments over Turkey's diversion of water from the two rivers that flow into Syria.
