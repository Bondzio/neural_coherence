<s> &lt;div&gt; Turn OFF the Personal Web Sharing, since this is not what your are trying to do. </s>
<s> Personal Web Sharing configures your Mac to essentially be a Web Server and gives you the ability to share files with anyone out there and is NOT associated with the sharing of your internet connection. </s>
<s> &lt;div&gt; Let's leave the Firewall off, for the time being and move to System Preferences &gt; Sharing and choose &quot;Internet Sharing&quot; Follow the instructions. </s>
<s> If chosen from here, the Firewall will be configured correctly when, or if, you decide to turn it back on. </s>
