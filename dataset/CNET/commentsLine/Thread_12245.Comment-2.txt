here's the deal: only one computer can make a wired connection per router port.
many computers can connect wirelessly, however.
you have to put a switch or hub or wireless access point on the other end of the cable to allow multiple computers to connect to the router through one of the numbered ports on the wrt54g.
internet ----- router ----- switch/hub/wap -~-~-~ computers ----- wired -~-~-~ wired or wirless
