<s> It would seem that something in your speaker system is acting as a detector/demodulator. </s>
<s> This could be a faulty component, a design flaw or any of a number of things. </s>
<s> A simple diode can act as a detector and some oscillating circuit act as a demodulator which just removes the carrier signal. </s>
<s> Even a poor ground or solder connection can act something like a diode depending on the circuit it's in. </s>
<s> An unintended oscillations in electronic circuits would be hard to isolate and resolve without proper instruments. </s>
<s> You can try shielding but my guess is your success will be limited. </s>
