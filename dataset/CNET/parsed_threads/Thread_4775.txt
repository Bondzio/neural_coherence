<s> One thing that puzzles me due to so many opinions is how much termal paste to apply to the CPU. </s>
<s> I'm definitely going to be using Artic Silver 5, the CPU will be an AMDAthlon64 X2 and the cooler fan will be a Zalman Flower. </s>
<s> As I understand it, to apply the paste I just place a seed sized blob on the cpu and let the pressure of the heatsink spread it around the cpu heat spreader. </s>
<s> In some instructions I have seen people covering the whole of the CPU case, where as most just have it spread over the area where the core is. </s>
<s> This is shown on the Artic Silver website... but only with older model chips. </s>
<s> As my CPU will be duel core, does that mean there are two cores inside the casing so it will be best to cover the whole of it? </s>
<s> or still just apply the paste to the central area? </s>
<s> You are correct about the heat spreader needing only a very small amount of grease at its center and allowing the sink pressure to spread it out on single core CPUs that have heat spreaders. </s>
<s> I would find a pictorial breakdown of the dual core CPU in question and determine the placement of the grease on it. </s>
<s> As long as you don't apply so much grease to the particular CPU that it oozes out of the sides and gets into circuitry I would not have trouble putting a very thin coat of grease covering the entire surface of the heat spreader if I were doing it. </s>
<s> One thing to look to is the original thermal material for the particular CPU. </s>
<s> Generally they have a thermal pad. </s>
<s> This thermal pad will be an indication of where to apply the grease if the grease maker doesn't provide precise details for a particular CPU. </s>
<s> http://www.arcticsilver.com/arctic_silver_instructions.htm </s>
<s> that the paste should be used for their &quot;lidded&quot; [their name for those they finally got around to putting a heat spreader on during manufacturing] CPU, and to use the Phase Change &quot;pad&quot; on the non lidded CPU's. </s>
<s> Something that escaped me that should have been obvious is the &quot;Heat spreader&quot;. </s>
<s> The heat from either a single core or dual core CPU will go to the heat spreader. </s>
<s> Going from the heat spreader to the heat sink would be the same for a dual core CPU as it would a single core CPU. </s>
<s> In other words, the instructions for applying grease to a heat spreader CPU is the same for a single or dual core CPU. </s>
<s> i prefer to use a plastic razor blade or the edge of a credit card to pre-spread the compound. </s>
<s> this is to make sure the entire die is covered and eliminate the chance of a hot-spot. </s>
<s> a small area with no compound is enough to kill a processor. </s>
