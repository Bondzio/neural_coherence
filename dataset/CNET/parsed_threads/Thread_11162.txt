<s> The dual router issue has been discussed here many times so I will just say hook a cable between the LAN ports on your routers and use the DLINK as the &quot;router&quot;. </s>
<s> Search for more details. </s>
<s> The more interesting requirement is that you want to use the same SSID. </s>
<s> This is how a corporate WLAN is done. </s>
<s> You can have them on the same channel or different but I would recommend different. </s>
<s> The PC will always choose the device with the strongest signal on a SSID.... it is actually very hard to get it to choose anything else. </s>
<s> This will in general work well. </s>
<s> The issues you have is if you move around or if for whatever reason the signal strength varies over time. </s>
<s> The PC many times will attempt to switch to another access point. </s>
<s> If you have WPA enabled it then must renegotiate the security and cause short outages during this time. </s>
<s> No big issue if it seldom happens but you must avoid it switching back and forth. </s>
<s> Most newer wireless clients have settings to control how often if ever it will switch. </s>
