hauppauage is the leading brand of tuners for the pc.
all of the cards/dongles can record atsc, analog ntsc, and qam signals.
wintv-hvr-1250 has a single analog/digital tuner and uses your cpu to do the mpeg encoding.
you can only watch what you're recording.
wintv-hvr-1600 has seperate analog/digital tuners and an onboard mpeg encorder.
it's a good choice for older pcs with less powerful cpus.
wintv-hvr-1800 is a revision of the hvr-1600 and includes fm radio.
wintv-hvr-2250 is the top of the line and has dual tuners so you can watch one program on your pc while recording another.
it can also record 2 different programs simultaneously.
