<s> voltages for ddr3-1333 can range from 1.5v to 1.8v. </s>
<s> the bios may be choosing a low voltage if the sticks differ. </s>
<s> the bios may also be choosing timings that are too aggressive for one of the pairs. </s>
<s> you can try to drop your memory speed, use relaxed timings, disable the Ai Transaction Booster, or manually set DRAM voltage. </s>
<s> you can do all of these from the Ai Tweaker BIOS menu. </s>
<s> to drop your memory speed, set DRAM Frequency=DDR3-1111/DDR3-1066 (whichever is available). </s>
<s> to use relaxed timings, set DRAM Timing Control= 9-9-9-24 (or the first settings with CAS Latency = 9). </s>
<s> to disable the booster, set Ai Transaction Booster=Disabled and Relax Level=5 . </s>
<s> to manually set the voltages, set Ai Overclock Tuner=Manual . </s>
<s> then scroll down and set DRAM Voltage=1.74/1.75 (or the highest voltage of your sticks + 0.08). </s>
<s> if one of the sticks requires 1.8V, do not use this tweak -- drop the memory speed instead. </s>
<s> all of these options except the voltage tweak will result in slightly lower performance. </s>
