<s> Any suggestions for wireless adapter for Dell Optplex 745 desktop? </s>
<s> You could try any brand-name USB adapter, IMHO. </s>
<s> Just make sure it's at least WPA capable. </s>
<s> Personally, I let windows XP software run the adapter, so it really doesn't matter a whole lot what adapter you but. </s>
<s> Sure, some will have a bit better range than others, but if you're well within range anyway, it won't matter. </s>
<s> Some models have a little &quot;stand&quot; to set the unit up so you get better reception. </s>
<s> Other cheaper ones don't. </s>
<s> Depends how close you are to the wifi router/AP as to whether or not you need a stand. </s>
<s> Here is a cheap TrendNet model that is low profile and liked: http://www.compusa.com/products/product_info.asp?product_code=50741847&pfp=browse&tabtype=rv#review You can also go with a PCI Adapter, if you have an open PCI port. </s>
<s> But I hear those can be a booger to get working sometimes. </s>
