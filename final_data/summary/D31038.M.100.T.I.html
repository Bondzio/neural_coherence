On August 7 , 1998 the U.S. embassies in Nairobi , Kenya and Dar es Salaam , Tanzania were bombed . A total of 259 , all but 12 Africans , were killed and more than 5400 , mostly in Nairobi , were injured . Two vehicles were used in each attack . The U.S. has tied these attacks to Osama bin Laden and his radical Islamic terrorist Al Qaeda organization . Immediate retaliations included cruise missile attacks on his camp in Afghanistan and a factory in the Sudan . By late October , a number of people had been charged , some arrested , and efforts to extradite others were continuing .
