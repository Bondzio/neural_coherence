<s> Your post doesn't tell of make or models so it appears you are not asking for help finding the drivers but just wanted to know why this is. </s>
<s> The reason that some of your hardware is not showing is simply because the Windows OS disk didn't have the drivers for them. </s>
<s> When you install the OS, it installs drivers that are on the CD for the hardware it knows about. </s>
<s> As XP was written some years ago, there is a good chance that your hardware is newer and hence there is no way that Microsoft could put the drivers on the CD. </s>
<s> What you need to do is to install the drivers from CD for the hardware that you have. </s>
<s> You do this after installing Windows. </s>
<s> I assume that you got the driver CDs when you bought the hardware. </s>
