<s> Also - you may have a dusty power supply and it's overheating as a result. </s>
<s> Try a can of compressed air to blow out the dust from inside the power supply. </s>
<s> Of course, you'll have to do this from the inside of the case. </s>
<s> So be sure to turn off the power supply or unplug it from the wall. </s>
<s> Then hit the start button on the computer to discharge any residual power. </s>
<s> Then ground yourself BEFORE you remove that side panel. </s>
<s> Otherwise you may short out that computer. </s>
<s> Then you'll be left with an expensive paperweight. </s>
