<s> Is it the encryption or the wireless connection itself or something else? </s>
<s> Yes, encryption does place some overhead on total throughput. </s>
<s> But it shouldn't cut throughput in half. </s>
<s> Furthermore, though you didn't tell us the details of your connection, even the slower 802.11b speeds (11Mbs nominal, 5-6Mbs real world) are significantly faster than most broadband connections, so the internet connection is often the limiting factor in download speed, not the wireless equipment. </s>
<s> If you want to test whether the encryption by itself is problematic, you could turn off encryption for a short while and see if the 2:1 download speed relationship persists between the wired desktop and the wireless laptop. </s>
<s> If the laptop is still a dog, you know it wasn't the encryption. </s>
<s> Check your signal levels, etc. </s>
<s> - a weak signal will cause slow transfer rates, with or without encryption. </s>
<s> You may need to tweak the positioning of your equipment to improve signal strength. </s>
<s> What else is running on your laptop? </s>
<s> Spyware/adware and P2P (even when you are not downloading anything yourself) are download speed killers. </s>
<s> If your laptop is functioning in a fundamentally different manner than the desktop, of course the download rates could be vastly different, unrelated to hardware or encryption. </s>
