<s> your motherboard supports dual channel operation so you should always use 2 or 4 dimms for best performance. </s>
<s> a pair of ddr-333 dimms in dual channel mode will outperform a single ddr-400 dimm. </s>
<s> some motherboards slow down to ddr-333 if all 4 slots are populated. </s>
<s> if you've 512MB (or less) using 2 DDR-333 dimmis, replace both and get 2 1GB DDR-400 dimms. </s>
<s> if you have one or more 512MB DDR-333 dimms, get additional 512MB DDR-333 dimms to reach 2GB. </s>
<s> 2GB of PC3200 (DDR400) RAM in dual channel mode wins. </s>
