<s> Even, under stress conditions it will do fine until it poops out. </s>
<s> When all is said and done, OEM based PCs are build to mirror their needs thus various models out there. </s>
<s> Those PCs that become stressed and they will generally under the &quot;logical boot sequence&quot; depend on the events of power-ON to happen. </s>
<s> If not meant, refuse to complete the cycle. </s>
<s> The results can be confusing at least but generally speaking a repeated attempts do result in a good boot. </s>
<s> Which in turn suggest a bad PSU or faltering one. </s>
<s> The other half is the mtrbd. </s>
