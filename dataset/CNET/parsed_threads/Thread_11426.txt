<s> Network = two or more computers connected together. </s>
<s> LAN = Local Area Network = just another name for a smaller network (such as the one you will create in your home). </s>
<s> You could connect two computers with one crossover network cable to form a two computer network (no hub, switch, or router needed). </s>
<s> Not very versatile, though. </s>
<s> Today, the simplest way to create a network is to get a router and connect all computers to it with network cable (not crossover cable). </s>
<s> If you get a broadband router, you can plug your Cable/DSL/other into it, so that all connected computers may share the Internet service. </s>
<s> Get a wireless broadband router (and compatible wireless network adapters) to add wireless connection capabilities. </s>
<s> If the Linux distribution has a proper driver for the network adapter installed in the computer, you shouldn't have problems. </s>
<s> Just connect all computers to a router. </s>
<s> Diagram: Internet --- DSL modem --- router --- (all computers connect to router) </s>
