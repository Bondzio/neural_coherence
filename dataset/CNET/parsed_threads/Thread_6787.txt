<s> Sometimes these &quot;wizards&quot; fail to work and you need to do things manually. </s>
<s> What you need to do is look at the router's documentation and find its default IP address. </s>
<s> From there, you set a static IP address in your PC that's within range of the router. </s>
<s> Example...if router's IP is 192.168.1.1, make your PC 192.168.1.2. </s>
<s> Then, open your browser and access the router using http:\\(router IP address). </s>
<s> This should bring you to it's web interface where you will have to enter a username/password. </s>
<s> There should be a default noted in your documentation. </s>
<s> Once you successfully enter these, you can make the changes you need. </s>
<s> Change the password as well. </s>
<s> Hope that helps. </s>
