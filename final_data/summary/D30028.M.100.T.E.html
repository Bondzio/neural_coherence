Tensions have increased between Turkey and Syria , bringing the two countries to the brink of war . Syria is displeased with Turkey 's growing relationship with Israel . Additionally , Syria has made territorial claims against Turkey and accused Turkey of diverting water from rivers that flow through both countries . Turkey accuses Syria of abetting Kurdish rebel activity in Turkey and granting a safe haven to Kurdish rebels . Turkey has raided Kurdish camps in Iraq and has massed troops on the Syrian border . In an effort to diffuse the crisis , Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak has flown to Syria and Turkey .
