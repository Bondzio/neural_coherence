<s> Either the hardware is not USB 2 functional or incorrect drivers are installed. </s>
<s> Make sure all drivers for the MB devices are up to date. </s>
<s> Sometimes Windows does not install the correct ones for some reason. </s>
<s> If the hardware is compatible and the USB 2 drivers are on the system but not installed, you can sometimes just remove and redetect the USB components. </s>
<s> Windows should find and use the best drivers. </s>
<s> Good luck. </s>
<s> Most of the USB ports that came on PIII systems less than 1Ghz were only USB 1.1. </s>
<s> To upgrade to USB 2 you can get an add-on PCI card. </s>
<s> My P4 system came with USB 1.1, so I picked up a five port USB 2 PCI card and put it in my computer. </s>
<s> It has four external ports and one internal port and cost less than $20 at Fry's. </s>
<s> If you are not afraid of working on your own system pick up a card, install the software that comes with it first, and then slipped the PCI card into any open slot on your motherboard. </s>
<s> Make sure you screw in tightly, move all your USB plugs to the new card and reboot your machine. </s>
<s> As long you as you use the new card you should not see that pesky message again. </s>
