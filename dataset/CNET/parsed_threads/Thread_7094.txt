<s> The heat sink and fan for the CPU are notorious dust collectors. </s>
<s> Removing this accumulation will often cause a considerable drop in CPU temperature. </s>
<s> You can use canned air as a simple remedy. </s>
<s> Some will suggest to disconnect the fan connection on the motherboard before spraying with air. </s>
<s> The reasoning is that some motors can also act as generators when spun by outside sources and can damage components. </s>
<s> When I clean mine, I remove the fan and use both air and a bristle brush (paint brush) to dislodge the dust. </s>
<s> I do the same with the fan blades. </s>
<s> You can also blow much of the dust from the power supply and other areas of the PC cabinet. </s>
<s> As for a noisy PS fan, these are more annoying than threatening. </s>
<s> They generally start by being noise when first turned on and then quieting a bit. </s>
<s> It's when they stop or run too slowly that they become a problem. </s>
<s> Most PS fans are the same size as standard 80mm case fans. </s>
<s> I've been able to replace many of these myself. </s>
<s> It's also possible to lube their bearing but that remedy isn't always long lasting. </s>
<s> As for replacing these fans, some come with 2 and some with three wires. </s>
<s> Three wire fans carry a signal that can interpret the fan's speed. </s>
<s> You'd want one that has the same number of wires as that being removed. </s>
<s> Replacing the fans generally requires cutting and splicing work so whether you'd want to try this or replace the whole CPU is determined by your own comfort level. </s>
<s> If you want to replace the PS, check the wattage on the old one first. </s>
<s> It's generally recommended to get one with a higher rating than the original. </s>
<s> To answer your question about a power supply damaging other components, the answer is that it can. </s>
<s> But, I don't think a noisy fan is a reliable indicator of imminent failure. </s>
<s> Happy Tday Well, you should replace the psu ASAP. </s>
<s> Don't drag your feet as the psu is the primary exhaust for the system. </s>
<s> Thus, when it starts to falter the effects are apparent as you notice the other fan pitches in. </s>
<s> So, find a decent psu and one with a higher wattage rating. </s>
<s> Many online vendors offer psus, like NewEgg, Geeks, and ZipZoomFly, so google for the website and review their offering. </s>
<s> While 400W+ is OK, you may find alot of 500W psus that suffice well. </s>
