<s> I replaced two of the three 512-MB sticks of RAM with two 1-GB sticks, increasing the total RAM from 1.5 GB to 2.5 GB. </s>
<s> (As mentioned earlier, the Sony specification for this PC is 1.5 GB maximum.) </s>
<s> When I started up the computer, the BIOS recognized all of the installed RAM, as &quot;2560 MB.&quot; However, the computer then produced a much louder operating sound--a loud whirring--than before. </s>
<s> It sounded like the system was overclocked and straining. </s>
<s> That obviously was not good. </s>
<s> After I had the PC shut down for about a half hour, I restarted it, and got an excessive temperature warning in BIOS. </s>
<s> Even after the 1/2-hour shutdown, the CPU temperature was at 76.5 degrees C and highlighted in red type--so the system had a lot of thermal inertia. </s>
<s> I decided not to endanger the PC further, so I removed the two 1-GB RAM sticks, and re-installed the original two 512-MB RAM sticks they had replaced, for a total of 1.5 GB of RAM. </s>
<s> Well, this was a learning experience! </s>
