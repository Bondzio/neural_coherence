Tensions between Turkey and Syria reached a stage of `` all but declared war . '' Turkey accuses Syria of harboring militant Kurdish rebels . Damascus denies sheltering Kurdish fighters . Turkey attacks Kurds in northern Iraq and masses troops on its Syrian border . Syria accuses Turkey of forming military and diplomatic alliances with Israel designed to subvert Syria and Arab states . Further , Syria accuses Turkey of diverting water from rivers that flow through both countries . Israel stresses that it has no part in the Turkey-Syria dispute . Egypt 's President Mubarak with Saudi and Arab support visits both countries to mediate the dispute and defuse tensions .
