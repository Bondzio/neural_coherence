<s> Hello, In reading these replies I can see that people are basing most of their answers on the name, rather than the quality of the system. </s>
<s> I have a very successful 'small chain' of computer stores in northern Michigan. </s>
<s> I see systems daily, I see customers daily, and I hear all the horror stories daily. </s>
<s> Read below for my recommendation: Let's start out with the parts game. </s>
<s> When you buy a BIG BOX system you have NO IDEA where the parts came from. </s>
<s> A RFB (request for bids) is issued many times a month by these companies for such things as motherboards, hard drives, etc. </s>
<s> The WINNER of these RFB's are the lowest priced supplier willing to build a part to the BIG BOX's specs. </s>
<s> Throw all these &quot;lowest bidders&quot; in a box to make a reliable PC? </s>
<s> Hmmm...and who made your motherboard anyway? </s>
<s> Now, quality. </s>
<s> Yea, most people are more than happy with their Dell, HP, eMachine, etc. </s>
<s> while it runs. </s>
<s> It's got a 1 year warranty, and unless you invested a fortune for an extended warranty with on-site repair, you need to box it up and send it back (a 2 week or more excurision for your system) just to get it fixed. </s>
<s> The systems arent put together all that well, it's an assembly line. </s>
<s> At the end of the line the QC folks (Quality Control) turn them on, and if they power up and burn in for a bit, they're OK to go. </s>
<s> That loose cable or badly bolted in motherboard issue won't show up until the folks in shipping drop the system while loading it on the truck anyway. </s>
<s> License? </s>
<s> What License? </s>
<s> When you but the BIG BOX system your Windows License (XP HOME or PRO) is tied to the BIOS chip identification number for your system. </s>
<s> If, let's say, 2 years down the road you have a thunderstorm and the motherboard is destroyed, you can send it to the manufacturer, and for a fee larger than what you paid for the system in the first place, they will replace the motherboard. </s>
<s> Or, you take it to the local computer shop, where your bill should be about $100.00 to replace the motherboard. </s>
<s> The issue is, you need to buy a new Windows license, yours won't work anymore since the BIOS chip isn't the same (the BIOS is on the Motherboard). </s>
<s> There are a hundred more problems with the BIG BOX's that I can point out, but that'll just bore the pants off everyone. </s>
<s> So let me make my suggestion here. </s>
<s> Build your own, and if you can't or don't know how, go to the local computer shop. </s>
<s> Let them build you a system that works for you. </s>
<s> It'll be customized to do what you want to do (just surf the net, play games, store mass quantities of music, etc.). </s>
<s> The warranties on these systems are usually manufacturers warranties, that's the way I do it. </s>
<s> CPU's and Motherboards are warranted for 5 years! </s>
<s> Other parts are usually 3 years, and the memory is or can be lifetime warranty. </s>
<s> License? </s>
<s> Windows XP HOME, PRO, whatever you want. </s>
<s> It will be OEM, which means, if the motherboard breaks you can replace it, not having to purchase another license, and Windows will continue to work (with a little massaging for the motherboard drivers if it's a different type). </s>
<s> Quality? </s>
<s> The quality of the systems built locally is MUCH higher than the BIG BOX, sure you pay a bit more, but it'll last. </s>
<s> I use name brand parts, no cheapies or refurbs. </s>
<s> I burn my systems in for 24 hours or more. </s>
<s> I make sure when that system leaves that the customer will not be back for a warranty issue. </s>
<s> I lose money when that happens (in labor, since I GIVE them that when something breaks). </s>
<s> BUT if your local built system does break, you have a desk to pound on, a place to visit, someone to yell at if need be. </s>
<s> I won't send you to India or some other offshore location for answers. </s>
