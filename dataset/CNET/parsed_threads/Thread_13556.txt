<s> they go into a standby mode and the pc still detects a monitor is attached to the port. </s>
<s> i recommend you boot the pc, delete both monitors in Device Manager, then shutdown. </s>
<s> disconnect the projector cable and move the monitor to the DVI port. </s>
<s> now boot up and then shutdown. </s>
<s> now add the projector and reboot. </s>
<s> the monitor should be your primary display while booting and the primary display in windows. </s>
