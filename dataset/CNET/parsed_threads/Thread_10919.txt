<s> By nothing you mean no power from the power supply, no fans spinning, no beeps from the internal speaker? </s>
<s> Check the power supply voltage switch is set to the correct line voltage. </s>
<s> Test the outlet the power cord is plugged into. </s>
<s> Check the power switch header on the motherboard for the correct orientation of the connector to the switch. </s>
<s> To test the power switch on the box, remove the connector from the header and short the header pins with a small screwdriver. </s>
<s> If the case switch is bad this should start the machine. </s>
<s> First time builders often try to install everything at once. </s>
<s> Instead, a test as you go approach is a good idea. </s>
<s> 1. </s>
<s> Motherboard, powersuppply, speaker and CPU+HeatSinkFan(HSF) will get you beeps. </s>
<s> The power switch is pushed and it usually beeps. </s>
<s> No beeps and you are looking at the possible misconfigured or bad parts. </s>
<s> 2. </s>
<s> Some system board come with the CMOS reset in the reset position. </s>
<s> Will not power up either. </s>
