<s> IDE (also called now PATA) is a wide 40 (or 80) conductor cable (Some cables with 80 conductors have the same 40 pins on each end.) </s>
<s> There can be possible 3 plugs (where a 2nd drive is connected to the middle connector - but that is NOT the reason for the 80 conductor possibility.) </s>
<s> SATA is a much smaller 4 wire cable with a small plug on each end. </s>
<s> Each cable can have only one drive connected. </s>
<s> The speed of SATA is faster than IDE. </s>
<s> The initial SATA (now called SATA I) was 150 MBytes/sec where the IDE was topped out at 133 MBytes/sec. </s>
<s> The newer SATA II is twice the speed of SATA I. </s>
<s> Additional reading: http://www.answers.com/sata </s>
