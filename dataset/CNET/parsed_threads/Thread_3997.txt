<s> Did you recently update your bios? </s>
<s> Sounds like the bios settings are in default and your memory is being check everytime you boot. </s>
<s> You can turn that off in the bios settings at boot, just hit the &quot;delete&quot; key during boot and the bios screen will come up. </s>
<s> Typically Non-VolatileRAM is used to store the BIOS code itself, as well as your BIOS settings. </s>
<s> Checking NVRAM generally consists of running a checksum on the NVRAM contents to determine if the contents have changed. </s>
<s> I have no idea why your BIOS is running a self check now where it seems it hasn't before, but it certainly should be harmless to let it run. </s>
<s> If the NVRAM is being held by the battery, it may be that your battery is low. </s>
<s> (although that is not usually the kind of NVRAM used for the BIOS) </s>
