<s> An optical drive cannot be installed on the floppy controller on the motherboard. </s>
<s> To remove the floppy, the floppy must be disabled in the BIOS. </s>
<s> Just removing it from device manager will cause it to be reinstalled on reboot. </s>
<s> Go into the BIOS and navigate to the floppy controller and disable it. </s>
<s> Power off and unplug the ribbon and power cable from the drive, and remove the drive. </s>
