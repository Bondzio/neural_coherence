<s> Hook up as follows ISP router/modem &gt; Netgear Now plug everything in and turn the isp modem on first. </s>
<s> Now turn on the Netgear router. </s>
<s> In the back on the router is a reset button. </s>
<s> Push it in for ten seconds and wait for it to reset. </s>
<s> Now plug in the ethernet cable to your computer. </s>
<s> Plug the other end of the ethernet cable into a numbered port of the router, 1,2,3 or 4. </s>
<s> Login to the router. </s>
<s> http://192.168.0.1 ( this will be your login, unless you change your LAN ip address. </s>
<s> If you change it then, click start, click run click command, type ipconfig /all and look for the default gateway. </s>
<s> this will be your new number to put in the address bar to log in.) </s>
<s> Once your in, You can either use the setup wizard or configure it yourself. </s>
<s> ftp://downloads.netgear.com/files/wgr614v4_install_guide.pdf Firmware update is found here http://kbserver.netgear.com/products/wgr614v4.asp Rick </s>
