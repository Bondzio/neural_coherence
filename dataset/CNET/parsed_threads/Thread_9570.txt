<s> I had to fix a customers HP Pavilion a305w Desktop PC! </s>
<s> Same thing happened as soon as I would connect the power cord to the tower it would turn on and then off. </s>
<s> So I ran some test and found out that the power switch was damaged. </s>
<s> Well the cables you might have to replace the cables that connect from the power button to the motherboard. </s>
<s> You might even have to buy a new front I/O power switch with cables. </s>
<s> It could also be that your power supply is dying out. </s>
<s> All in all luckily for me all I had to do it replace the front I/O power switch with a new set and that worked. </s>
<s> Customer is happy. </s>
<s> So yeah that might be the problem with that PC. </s>
