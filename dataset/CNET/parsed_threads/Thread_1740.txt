<s> Alright, you are having a drive conflict, so here's what you need to do: 1.) </s>
<s> Click START, and then right click on MY COMPUTER. </s>
<s> Select MANAGE -- this will bring up the COMPUTER MANAGEMENT WINDOW. </s>
<s> 2.) </s>
<s> On the pane furthest to the left in the COMPUTER MANAGEMENT window, there should be an option for DEVICE MANAGER. </s>
<s> Click on DEVICE MANAGER. </s>
<s> 3.) </s>
<s> Now, the DEVICE MANAGER should open in the middle pane of the COMPUTER MANAGEMENT window. </s>
<s> Click on the arrow next to the DISK DRIVES option in the middle pane. </s>
<s> Make sure that both External Hard Drives. </s>
<s> 4.) </s>
<s> With the DISK DRIVES option selected, back over on the left hand pane of the COMPUTER MANAGEMENT window, select DISK MANAGEMENT. </s>
<s> DISK MANAGEMENT will now open up in the middle pane of the COMPUTER MANAGEMENT window. </s>
<s> 5.) </s>
<s> In the DISK MANAGEMENT window, scroll down until you see your unrecognized EXTERNAL HARD DRIVE. </s>
<s> It will be showing a status of OFFLINE. </s>
<s> You need to right click on this option and select GO ONLINE. </s>
